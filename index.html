The "cute segregation sim" was based off the work of Nobel Prize-winning game theorist, Thomas Schelling. Specifically, his 1971 paper, Dynamic Models of Segregation. They originally built on top of this, and showed how a small demand for diversity can desegregate a neighborhood. In other words, they gave his model the ol' "happy ending."

If you like to educate yourself instead of being spoonfed, read up on "diversity and social cohesion."
